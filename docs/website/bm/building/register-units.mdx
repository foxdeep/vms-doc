# Description

On the "Register Units" page, users can view a table of all the registered units for the building. Being a registered unit means that the unit is authorized to apply for tasks and tickets to access the building, view the list of contractors and personnel, and receive announcements that have been released.

## Invite

To invite a unit, follow these steps:

- Click on the "Invite" button located at the lower-right corner of the page.
- A dialog box will appear containing the following fields:

  - Name
  - Username
  - Email

- Fill in the required fields and click "Invite" to invite the unit.

## Delete

To delete a unit from the building, users can click on the "More" button to open the menu and then click on the "Delete" button.
