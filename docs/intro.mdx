# What is VMS?

**Visitor Management System (VMS)**, a system for managing visitors.
In general, anyone who is <mark>not a full-time employee</mark> is considered a **visitor**.
Proper arrangements and management should be made for visitors entering and leaving buildings (factories/offices) and engaging in any activities.
A universal platform for office automation is provided to effectively manage records of visitor activities in and out.

There are few universal VMS systems available on the market, most of which are company-specific registration systems (or <mark>paper-based</mark>).
The Cloud Platform aims to provide a SaaS service that establishes a management service applicable to both internal enterprises and general commercial offices.

## Why you need

- Grasp visitor flow
- Rapid identity confirmation
- Paperless and eco-friendly environment
- Manage activity records
- Ensure visitor/employee safety

### Benifits

- 🚀 Increased efficiency
  - Provides rapid visitor identity confirmation for quicker entry, saving time.
- ♻️ Environmentally friendly
  - Digitalized processes reduce paper usage, ESG-friendly.
- 📈 Reports
  - Provides users with downloadable report data.
- 🗄️ Safety
  - Apart from data security, quick personnel management responses provide greater safety for users.
- 🔍 Query
  - Historical data is available for users to query at any time.
- 📆 Appointment
  - Utilizes a pre-appointment system that is systematic and automated.
